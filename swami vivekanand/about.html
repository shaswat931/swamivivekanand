About Swami Vivekanand Vidyalay

Our History

Swami Vivekanand Vidyalay was established in 1990 by Shri Laxmanrao Patil to provide quality education. We have nurtured thousands of students with knowledge and values.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To empower students with knowledge and character to become responsible citizens.

Mission: To provide holistic education that fosters academic excellence and ethical values.
