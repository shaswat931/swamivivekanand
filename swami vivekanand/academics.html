Curriculum Overview

Our curriculum is designed to meet national standards and prepares students for the challenges of the modern world. It includes a perfect blend of academic knowledge, practical skills, and moral values.

Nursery & Kindergarten

Play-based learning with focus on early literacy and numeracy skills.

Primary (Class 1–5)

Strong foundation in reading, writing, mathematics, and science.

Middle School (6–8)

Focus on conceptual clarity, group activities, and project-based learning.

Secondary & Sr. Sec (9–12)

Board exam preparation, career counseling, lab-based science, and competitive coaching.

Streams Offered

  • 📘 Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, Computer Science)
  • 📗 Commerce (Accounting, Economics, Business Studies, IT)
  • 📙 Arts (History, Geography, Political Science, Sociology)

Assessment System

We follow a continuous and comprehensive evaluation system. Students are assessed through regular tests, assignments, project work, and term exams to ensure all-round development.
